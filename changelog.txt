Version 3.39 (Build 69)
----------------------
[New Features]
- Meeting Mode: Voice-activated meeting recording (Say "Meeting", "Record", or "Pulong").
- AI Meeting Minutes: Automatic professional summary generation using Gemini 1.5 Flash.
- Trilingual Support: Ashlee now understands English, Tagalog, and Bisaya (Cebuano) simultaneously.
- Offline Mode: Automatically saves raw meeting transcripts as .txt files when Wi-Fi is unavailable, ensuring no data loss.
- Document Auto-Correction: Enabled perspective correction and auto-cropping in the scanner for perfectly aligned, professional results.

[Improvements]
- Long-Meeting Reliability: Patched voice recognition to automatically recover from timeouts and "busy" errors during sessions up to 8 hours.
- Refined Naming Dialog: Removed auto-prompting for a quieter manual entry experience; voice feedback now only triggers on action buttons (Save/Share/Cancel).
- Search Indexing: Meeting minutes are now automatically indexed into the internal database for instant searchability.

[Fixes]
- Fixed duplicate context import conflicts.
- Optimized TTS feedback loop guards to prevent Ashlee from hearing herself.
