Pryce Scan v3.22 (52) - Changelog

[New Voice AI Features]
• Interactive Naming: You can now name your documents using your voice! Just say the name when prompted after a scan.
• Voice Command Shortcuts: 
    - Say "Yes", "Save", or "Itago" to save your document.
    - Say "PDF" or "JPEG" to switch formats instantly via voice.
• Advanced Conversion Commands: Directly trigger conversions like "Word to PDF" or "PDF to JPEG" using voice commands while the converter is open.
• Navigation: Added "Open View Saved Documents" and "Open Merge Multiple PDFs" voice triggers.

[Ashlee AI Improvements]
• Auto-Resume: Ashlee now automatically starts listening again after she finishes responding to you.
• Smarter Context: Ashlee now understands where you are (e.g., in a naming dialog or converter) and adjusts her hearing to match.
• Language Support: Improved Tagalog recognition for all major commands.

[System & Stability]
• Enhanced Update Engine: Optimized the check-for-updates logic to be faster and more reliable.
• UI Polish: Updated the version branding and layout stability in the main dashboard.
• Bug Fixes: Resolved an issue where the voice recognizer would stay "busy" and stop responding to commands.
