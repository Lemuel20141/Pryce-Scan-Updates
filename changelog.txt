Pryce Scan v3.23 (53) - Changelog

[New Update System]
• Live Progress Tracking: Added a real-time progress bar when downloading updates so you know exactly how much is left.
• Auto-Install: The app now automatically opens the installer as soon as the update download finishes.

[File Converter Voice Support]
• Direct Conversion Commands: While the converter dialog is open, you can now say:
    - "JPG to PDF" or "Larawan sa PDF"
    - "PDF to JPEG" or "PDF sa Larawan"
    - "Word to PDF" or "Dokumento sa PDF"
    - "PDF to Word" or "PDF sa Dokumento"
• Ashlee will confirm the selection and immediately open the correct file picker.

[Ashlee AI UI/UX Enhancements]
• Floating Action Button: Added the "Ask Ashlee" button (FAB) for quick access to voice commands.
• Smart Visibility: The Ashlee button now automatically hides when Voice AI is disabled and reappears when enabled.
• Status Icons: The AI control button now shows a microphone icon when active and a "silent" icon when off.
• Auto-Resume: Ashlee now intelligently restarts listening mode after she finishes speaking her response.

[System & Stability]
• Fixed a bug where the voice recognizer would stay "busy" and fail to trigger after the first command.
• Improved memory management during heavy PDF merging and conversion processes.
• Resolved build synchronization issues between the Voice AI engine and the main UI.
