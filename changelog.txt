Pryce Scan v3.25 (55) - Changelog

[Voice AI - Ashlee Enhancements]
• Multi-Step Naming: Ashlee now guides you through a smarter workflow:
    1. Ask for Document Name.
    2. Ask for Format (PDF or JPEG).
    3. Ask for Action (Save, Share, or Cancel).
• Fixed "Echo" Bug: Prevented Ashlee from hearing herself and repeating the "Starting Scanner" command.
• Improved Listening: Added a "Heard" visual toast and fixed logic to ensure she waits for your specific choice before proceeding.
• Permission Recovery: Added a manual permission check and requester for the microphone to ensure she never stops listening due to system blocks.

[Smart Naming Dialog]
• Integrated Sharing: Added a "Share" button directly in the naming dialog for instant document distribution.
• Smart View: The "View Saved Documents" button now intelligently opens your selected Local folder in the File Manager or your Google Drive folder in the Drive app.

[Seamless Updates]
• One-Tap Install: Renamed "Download" to "Update Now" for better clarity.
• Auto-Installer: The app now automatically launches the installer once the download hits 100%.
• Permission Guide: Added a smart shortcut to Android Settings if "Install Unknown Apps" is required.

[Stability]
• Fixed lifecycle crashes related to the speech engine when rotating or leaving the app.
• Optimized memory usage during high-resolution JPEG to PDF conversions.
