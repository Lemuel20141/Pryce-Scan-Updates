# Privacy Policy for Pryce Scan

Last updated: [Current Date]

Pryce Scan ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use information when you use our mobile application.

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
Pryce Scan is designed to be a privacy-first tool. 
- **Google Drive Access:** The app requests access to your Google Drive via the `drive.file` scope. This allows the app to ONLY see and manage files that were created by the app itself. We cannot see your other personal files.
- **Local Storage:** The app saves documents to the folders you specify on your device.
- **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, scan data, or account credentials on our own servers. All data stays between your device and your Google Drive account.

### 2. Third-Party Services
We use Google Play Services for authentication and Google Drive API for storage. These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

### 3. Permissions
The app requires Internet access to upload scans to Google Drive and Storage permissions to save files locally.
